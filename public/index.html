How It Works

Setting up your SASTA CCTV security network takes less than two minutes. All you need are two devices connected to the internet.

1

Create Account

Register a secure account. Use this account to link all cameras and monitors together safely.

2

Deploy Cameras

Open SASTA CCTV on your old device (phone or laptop), choose **Camera Console**, and position the device.

3

Watch Live

Open the **Web Monitor** on your primary phone or PC to watch live feeds and receive real-time motion alerts.
